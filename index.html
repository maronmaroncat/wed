COLD CLIMINATE

Boston is in the northeastern part of the United States, at 42 degrees longitude and 71 degrees latitude.　 It is located at about the same latitude as Hakodate in Japan. Winter in the Boston is very cold. Especially, January is the coldest month of the year. It is said the lowest temperature is minus 5 degrees Celsius and the higtest temperature is 3 degrees Celsius.
